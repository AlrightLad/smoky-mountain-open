How this works: Mark each amendment as approve / reject / defer. Decisions are stored in your browser only. When done, click Export amendments to download amendments-<ts>.json with kind: "amendments" — the Downloads watcher routes it to apply-amendments.sh. Approved amendments are applied to their target_canonical_path per their type (new-file / replace-existing / append-to-existing / edit-section). Drafts then move from .claude/state/amendments/pending/ to applied/ (or rejected/ / deferred/).
