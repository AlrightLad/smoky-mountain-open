# The Free-Forever Core — written governance

**Status:** Draft for Founder ratification · authored 2026-06-11 (strategy
brief action #4: "codify free-forever core as written governance before any
monetization work").
**Authority:** This document binds product decisions once the Founder
ratifies it. Until then it is the working draft the orchestration team
builds against.

## The promise

PARBAUGHS' core is free, forever, for every member. "Core" means the things
a golf league needs to exist:

1. **Logging rounds** — solo or league, hole-by-hole or totals, with
   handicap math. Never metered, never capped.
2. **Belonging to leagues** — joining, standings, seasons, records, the
   roster. No per-member fee, no league-size paywall on the basics.
3. **The Clubhouse** — chat, tee times, events, the feed. Community is the
   product; charging for it would be charging for the point.
4. **ParCoin** — granted by play, spendable on cosmetics. Per the standing
   model (see project decision, 2026-06): cash may one day buy ParCoins
   (one-way, no cash-out, cosmetic-only spend) but coins are never required
   for anything in the core, and nothing of real-world value ever comes out.
5. **Your data** — export (backup codes) and account deletion remain free
   and unconditional. Data is never held hostage behind a tier.

## What MAY ever be paid (the fence around monetization)

If monetization ships, it is limited to:

- **Cosmetics** — themes, badges, flair, bought with ParCoin (which cash may
  top up, one-way). Never gameplay or standings advantage (the
  not-gambling posture in `parbaughs-legal-compliance` §1 depends on this).
- **Patron-style support** — voluntary support of the platform with
  cosmetic recognition only.
- **Genuinely new, costly capabilities** that do not exist today and whose
  marginal cost is real (e.g., bulk media storage) — and only with a
  Founder-ratified amendment to this document naming the feature.

## What may NEVER be paid

- Anything in the five core items above.
- Anything a member already had for free (no clawbacks behind a paywall).
- Visibility or fairness: standings, handicaps, and records are never
  influenced by spending of any kind.

## Why this is written down

Trust is the platform's only moat with a 20-person founding league and an
invite-only growth model. A pricing change that breaks this document would
spend that trust; the document exists so the cost of that decision is
visible BEFORE it is made. Changes require: a Founder decision recorded in
`.claude/state/founder-decisions/`, a Caddy Note telling members plainly,
and a version bump of this document with the old text preserved below it.
