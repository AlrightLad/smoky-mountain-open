Escalations

Founder review queue for escalations the orchestration team can't resolve without vision-level or strategic input. Mirrors the amendments + proposals lifecycle: pending → Founder decision → watcher applies via apply-escalations.sh.

How this works: Each escalation surfaces the team's proposed answer (per AMD-015) + options + decision form. Tick the option(s) you select OR write a custom answer. Mark each escalation as approve / reject / defer. Decisions are stored in your browser only. When done, click Export escalations to download escalations-<ts>.json with kind: "escalations" — the Downloads watcher routes it to apply-escalations.sh, which moves files from pending/ to approved/ / rejected/ / deferred/ with the decision captured. The team picks up approved escalations on the next agent loop and moves them to applied/ when implementation lands.
