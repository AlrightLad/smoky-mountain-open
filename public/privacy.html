Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Parbaughs ("we", "us", "our") operates the Parbaughs mobile application and web app. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Account info: Email address, username, display name
  • Profile info: Profile photo, bio, home course, club distances (all optional)
  • Golf data: Round scores, hole-by-hole stats (FIR, GIR, putts), range sessions, course reviews
  • Social data: Chat messages, DMs, wagers, bounties, league memberships
  • Device data: FCM push notification token (if you opt in), browser type

We do NOT collect GPS location, contacts, call logs, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter in the app.

How We Use Your Data

  • Display your profile, scores, and stats to your league members
  • Calculate handicaps, season standings, and achievements
  • Send push notifications you opted into (DMs, round alerts, event reminders)
  • Improve the app experience

Data Storage

All data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore) hosted in the United States. Firebase is SOC 2 and ISO 27001 certified.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data to anyone. Period. We do not run ads. We do not use ad tracking. Your data is shared only with your league members as part of normal app functionality.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing support@parbaughs.golf. Account deletion is processed within 30 days and removes your member profile, round history, chat messages, and all associated data.

Children's Privacy

Parbaughs is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us for immediate removal.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy: support@parbaughs.golf
