Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Parbaughs ("we", "us", "our") operates the Parbaughs mobile application and web app. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Account info: Email address, username, display name
  • Profile info: Profile photo, bio, home course, club distances (all optional)
  • Golf data: Round scores, hole-by-hole stats (FIR, GIR, putts), range sessions, course reviews
  • Social data: Chat messages, DMs, wagers, bounties, league memberships
  • Device data: FCM push notification token (if you opt in), browser type
  • Location (optional, opt-in): If you tap "Use my current location," your browser shares your approximate coordinates (we request low-accuracy, city-level location, not precise GPS). You can also set your location manually by typing a city and state. We store this only to show local weather and find nearby courses. You can change or remove it at any time in Settings.

We do NOT collect your contacts, call logs, precise GPS movement tracking, advertising identifiers, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter or opt into in the app. We never track your location in the background.

How We Use Your Data

  • Display your profile, scores, and stats to your league members
  • Calculate handicaps, season standings, and achievements
  • Send push notifications you opted into (DMs, round alerts, event reminders)
  • Show local weather and nearby courses (only if you provide a location)
  • Improve the app experience

Data Storage

All data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore) hosted in the United States. Firebase is SOC 2 and ISO 27001 certified.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data to anyone. Period. We do not run ads. We do not use ad tracking. Your data is shared only with your league members as part of normal app functionality.

Third-Party Services

We use a small number of trusted service providers to run the app. They process data only as needed to provide their service:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, hosting, and push notifications (United States).
  • GolfCourseAPI — course and par data lookup when you search for a course.
  • Open-Meteo — weather forecasts for your saved location.
  • Sentry — anonymized error reporting to help us fix crashes. We scrub personal data from error reports.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time, directly in the app: go to Settings → Delete My Account. This immediately and permanently removes your member profile, round history, stats, chat messages, location, and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

You can also request deletion by emailing support@parbaughs.golf, and we will process it within 30 days.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under privacy laws such as the GDPR (Europe) or CCPA/CPRA (California), including the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correct — fix inaccurate data (most of which you can edit yourself in Settings).
  • Delete — remove your account and data (available in-app, see above).
  • Object or restrict — limit how we use your data.

We do not sell your personal information and do not use it for targeted advertising. To exercise any right, email support@parbaughs.golf.

Children's Privacy

Parbaughs is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us for immediate removal.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
