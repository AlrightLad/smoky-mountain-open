Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a league?
Go to More → My Leagues → Create a League. Choose a name, location, and visibility. You become the commissioner and can invite friends with a unique invite code.
How do I invite friends?
Share your league's invite code (found on the league detail page). They enter it during registration or on the Join a League page. You can also share invite links directly.
How do ParCoins work?
ParCoins are earned by playing golf (50 coins per 18-hole round), hitting the range (10 coins), and daily logins (1 coin). Spend them on cosmetic items in the Shop — rings, banners, card themes, name effects, and titles. ParCoins have zero real-world cash value.
How is my handicap calculated?
We use the World Handicap System (WHS) formula. Your score differential is calculated as (113 / Slope) × (Score - Rating). We take your best differentials from your most recent 20 rounds. You need at least 3 rounds for a handicap. 9-hole rounds are paired chronologically.
How do seasons work?
Three seasons per year: Spring (Mar-May), Summer (Jun-Aug), Fall (Sep-Nov). December through February is off-season — your rounds still count for handicap but don't earn season points. The season champion earns the Champion Red theme.
Can I be in multiple leagues?
Yes! League membership is unlimited and free. Your ParCoins, cosmetics, and achievements travel with you across all your leagues.
How do wagers work?
Challenge a league member to a head-to-head wager. Both players put up ParCoins (held in escrow). When both play their rounds, the winner takes the pot. Wager types include stroke play, best 9, most pars, fewest putts, and Nassau.
How do I change my theme?
Go to Settings → Theme and tap any theme card. There are 8 themes to choose from. Champion Red is unlocked by winning a season or event.
How do I share my scorecard?
After finishing a round, tap Share on the round detail page. A branded scorecard image is generated and can be shared to any app — Instagram, iMessage, WhatsApp, etc.
How do I contact support?
Email us at support@parbaughs.golf. We'll get back to you within 48 hours.
