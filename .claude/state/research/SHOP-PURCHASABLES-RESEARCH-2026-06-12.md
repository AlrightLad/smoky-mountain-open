# Shop purchasables — research brief (2026-06-12)

> Founder ask (#43): "deeper store research (video-game + market comparison)
> for better purchasables." Researched cosmetic-economy design (games + golf
> apps) and filtered everything through PARBAUGHS values: **play-earned,
> cosmetic-only, non-gambling, community-over-competition.**

## What the research says drives cosmetic value

1. **Identity / self-expression is the #1 driver.** Players buy to *stand out
   among friends* — to signal who they are. For a 20-person friend league this
   is the strongest lever of all (everyone knows everyone; a cosmetic is a
   personality statement, not anonymous flexing).
2. **Earned > bought for status.** GolfN's "certified achievements" and Golf
   With Your Friends' unlock-through-play both show items that gate behind a
   real feat carry status a paid skin never does. PARBAUGHS' play-earned
   ParCoin model is already aligned — lean into it.
3. **Rarity tiers that feel distinct.** Common→Legendary works when each tier
   reads differently. (We already have range/proshop/locker/cabinet — audit
   that the top tier is genuinely aspirational, not just a pricier dot.)
4. **Customization depth/breadth.** More surfaces to personalize = more
   engagement. (We have border/banner/card/title/marker/voice — strong base.)
5. **Avoid FOMO + randomness.** The literature flags countdown timers and
   loot-box randomness as exploitative — and they'd pressure our non-gambling
   posture (Legal §1). No timed-pressure, no random boxes, no pay-to-win.
   Cosmetic-only, transparent price, no real-world value.

## Proposed "better purchasables" (ranked, all values-safe)

### A. Achievement-gated cosmetics (highest status, $0 — earned not bought)
The strongest lever: cosmetics you *can't buy*, only earn by real golf.
- **Sub-90 Club** ring/title — first 18-hole round under 90.
- **Nemesis Slayer** badge — flip a losing head-to-head to winning.
- **Trip Champion** frame — win a Smoky-Mountain-Open-style event.
- **Course Record** holder flair — already a theme; mirror as a worn badge.
- **Ironman** — log a round every month of the season.
These feed the existing "earnedBy / Not for sale" cabinet tier and make the
shop a trophy case, not just a paint store.

### B. Group / social cosmetics (community-over-competition)
- **Team banners / crests** for scramble squads (ties to the scramble-team
  work shipped v8.25.6–.9 — a squad that plays together gets a shared mark).
- **"Played together" markers** — a cosmetic that unlocks after N rounds with
  the same partner (rewards the social graph, not solo grinding).
- **League-anniversary frame** — for founding members / tenure.

### C. Deeper personalization surfaces (new categories to spend on)
- **Scorecard themes** — restyle the live-round scorecard (paper/felt/blueprint).
- **Profile-card layouts** — beyond banner color, a couple of card structures.
- **Caddie voices** — already 4 (Birdie added v8.25.6); Bag Room Guy is the
  paid one. Room for 1–2 more *earned* voices (e.g., a "Legend" voice unlocked
  by a hole-in-one).

### D. Seasonal, NON-FOMO
- **"2026 Season" frame** earned by playing N rounds in-season (participation,
  not a countdown). Resets each season as a soft collectible, never pressured.

## Recommendation
Shift the shop's center of gravity toward **A (earned)** and **B (social)** —
they're free, they reinforce community-over-competition, they sidestep every
gambling/FOMO risk, and they're the items a tight friend group actually wants
(status among people who know you). Keep paid ParCoin cosmetics as pure
self-expression flair (C), never status-by-purchase. This also strengthens the
not-gambling posture (Legal §1): the most desirable items have no purchase path
at all.
